Why Choose Us?

Featuring a selection of Italian, Mediterranean, French, Tex-Mex and Far-Eastern delicacies along with Indian regional favourites, The Earth Plate is Sahara Star's global cuisine restaurant. One of the largest all-day dining restaurants in the city, you can watch the culinary maestros at work in the show kitchen while you enjoy delicacies from around the world. The Earth Plate also brings to India for the first time Teppanyaki ice cream, an indulgence whipped up right before your eyes.
